REVIEW

Ian Brownville April 28th, 2026

Amadeus is a drama about rivalry and jealousy centered around Wolfgang Amadeus. Mozart, the protagonist, and Antonio Salieri. The movie has strong, phenomenal acting performances. with amazing storytelling, a retelling of Mozart's life in Austria.
